Appweb Roadmap

The Appweb roadmap is created by listening to user input. As such, it is subject to change. This roadmap is not a commitment to product, but reflects our current thinking about future directions. Those who shout the loudest — often get what they ask for. To request a feature, please send email to: dev@embedthis.com

What's in the Next Release

The next release will be the Embedthis Appweb 4.3.0. Patches are typically released incrementally as issues emerge.

Appweb 2 and Appweb 3 are now regarded as legacy code. Appweb 2 is officially deprecated. Customers should therefore upgrade to Appweb 4 as soon as possible.
